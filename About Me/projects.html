Computer Vision with Ronaldo and Messi

(LinkedIn)

  • During the World Cup 2022, I practiced YOLOv5 to recognize Cristiano Ronaldo and Lionel Messi.

  • First, I labeled a total of 100 images of Ronaldo and Messi in Makesense.ai, an open-source annotation tool. Afterward, I separated these images into train and validation data sets. Finally, I trained the data through YOLOv5 and implemented it in a YouTube video to recognize Messi and Ronaldo.

Sentiment Analysis using GRU and BERT

  • Applied NLP for sentiment analysis on "hespiburada.com", which is one of the most popular e-commerce companies in Turkey.

  • I used first Gated Recurrent Unit (GRU), then the Bert model to predict negative (class 1) and positive (class 0) customer reviews. In both models, recall test scores for class 1 varied between 77% and 92%. The average precision score was 73% and 89% in GRU and Bert models respectively.

Prediction of Car Prices using Decision Trees, Random Forest and Boosting Models (AdaBoost, GradientBoost, XGBoost)

GitHub: Decision Trees vs Random Forest

GitHub: Comparison of the Boosting Models

  • I worked on the Car dataset consisting of 301 observations and 8 features. For this regression problem, first I used Decision Trees with the best hyperparameters and obtained over 0.93 R2 and 1.37 the Root Mean Square Error (RMSE) scores. Then, I applied Random Forest and got 0.973 R2 and 0.851 error scores.

  • Afterward, I implemented popular Boosting Models such as AdaBoost, GradientBoost and XGBoost. AdaBoost model provided 0.94 R2 and 1.27 RMSE scores. I could improve the R2 score (0.95) and reduce the error (1.1) with GradientBoosting. Finally, I tried the XGBoost model and received the highest R2 (0.973) and the lowest error (0.847) scores.

Cancer Prediction using the K-Nearest Neighbors Algorithm (KNN)

(GitHub)

  • Based on observations in two different genes, I predicted the presence of cancer through the KNN algorithm. I split the data as 70% train and 30% test size and tried different K values to receive better scores. After using Pipeline and GridSearchCV, KNN provided a 93% accuracy score. Also, I got 93% and 92% F1-test scores for class 0 and class 1 respectively in this dataset.

(GitHub)
